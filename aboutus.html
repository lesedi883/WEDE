About Us

we provide the ultimate beauty experience for our clients, from hair and makeup to nails and aesthetic all under one roof. The hairstyling space is light, spacious and welcoming with cappuccio's on top. the decor is all about neutral tones such as white, grey and black with touches of rose gold and blush hues. The fountain is the ultimate tranquil retreat. All our styling services include a head massage done in our tropical room where you can relax to the soothing sound of meditation music. Our highly skilled stylists are masters at their craft from creative colours and balayages to fabulous bouncy blowout's. We are available monday to saturday includimg public holidays to ensure we are available at time that suits you. We look forward to welcoming you soon.
